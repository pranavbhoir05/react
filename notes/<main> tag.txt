Without <main>:

<header></header>
<div>content</div>
<footer></footer>
Browser cannot clearly identify where the primary content begins.



With <main>:

<header></header>
<main>content</main>
<footer></footer>

Systems can:

skip navigation directly to main content (accessibility)
understand page hierarchy (SEO)
maintain consistent layout structure in frameworks like React.

Core function: semantic identification of the page’s primary content region.
